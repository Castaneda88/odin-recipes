Description

This recipe is one of my favorites. May be cut in half or fours depending on preference. Pairs great with a glass of milk.
Serves 1, Prep time 5 min

Note:

Preserves may be substituted with any flavor of your choice

Ingrediandts

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon of grape preserves

Steps

  1. Place slices of bread next to each other matching opposite ends to each other
  2. Add preserves to the slice on the right covering end to end, corner to corner. Ensuring all preserves have covered the entire slice of bread
  3. next add peanut butter in the same fashion to the adjacent slice of bread
  4. Combine the two slices, touching the two sides with both peanut butter and jelly to each other forming a sandwich
  5. enjoy with a large glass of milk
