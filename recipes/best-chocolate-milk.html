Description

Fruitcake muffin danish I love chocolate. Pudding gummies soufflé jelly-o shortbread. Pastry apple pie biscuit tiramisu cake. Tart toffee tootsie roll gummies croissant dragée.

Ingrediants

  1. 16 oz Whole Milk
  2. 4 1/2 tablespoons of Hershey's Chocolate Syrup

Steps

  • In a small pot, heat milk to 160 F
  • With a whisk, stir milk to prevent churning
  • Remove milk from heat just before it begins to boil
  • In a glass cup, add Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
  • Begin adding warmed milk to cup, mixing with a teaspoon
  • Mix thoroughly to incorporate all the chocolate to the milk
  • Refrigerate for 2 hours
  • Enjoy!
