Description

Easily the most underrated iteration of this amazing childhood past time.
This recipe yields 16 fl oz and can be split to serve 2, prep time 2-3 hours

Ingrediants

  • 16 oz Whole Milk
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons of Hershey's Chocolate Syrup

Steps

  1. In a small pot, heat milk to 160 F
  2. With a whisk, stir milk to prevent churning
  3. Remove milk from heat just before it begins to boil
  4. In a glass cup, add Hershey's Chocolate Syrup
  5. Begin adding warmed milk to cup, mixing with a teaspoon
  6. Mix thoroughly to incorporate all the chocolate to the milk
  7. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours
  8. Enjoy!
