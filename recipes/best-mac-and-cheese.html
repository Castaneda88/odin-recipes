Ingredients

  1. 16 oz elbow pasta
  2. 2 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  3. 6 oz heavy cream
  4. 8 oz sharp cheddar cheese
  5. 2 tablespoons sour cream
  6. 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Steps

  • In a large pot bring to boil 3 quarts of water
  • In a medium saucepan, clarify butter on medium low for 15 minutes
  • Using a grader, shred cheese and place on side
  • Once water has come to a boil add pasta and allow to cook until no longer firm, or 8 to 12 minutes
  • Add heavy cream to butter and bring heat down to low for 15 minutes
  • Drain pasta in a colander and allow to sit, do not rinse
  • Raising heat to med, gradually add cheese to sauce pan, mix well together until all cheese is melted and incorporated into cheese sauce 8 to 15 minutes than remove from heat
  • Place pasta back into pot and add cheese sauce to pot
  • With heat on low, add sour cream to pot for 3 - 5 minutes.
  • Add salt to taste
  • Enjoy
